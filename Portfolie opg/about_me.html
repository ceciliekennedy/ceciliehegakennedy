About me



Hello and welcome to my site.

My name is Cecilie Hega Kennedy and i am 22 years old. Im born and raise in Denmark, Copenhagen but have some roots from Portugal.

I love to be creative and that is reflected in my work as a Makeup-artist, Multimediadesigner & SoMe Manager.
I have been a makeupartist for almost five years now and I have worked for Bobbi Brown Cosmetics, Gucci Beauty as a Manager and as an freelance makeupartist.

As a person i am very driven by doing things i love and i always reach after my goals. I love details and the very clean, romatic and classick look with an edge.
