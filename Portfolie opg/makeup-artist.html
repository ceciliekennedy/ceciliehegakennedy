My work

Beauty is the place where perfectionism reigns, because when you look close enought its so important that the makeup looks beautiful and flawless. Beauty is like a panting, where the face is canvas and that is what i love about makeup. At the end of the day you can wash it of and you will still be you.
I love the details and i spend a lot of time on blending and getting the look perfect. I have done a lot of fashion shows & photoshoots through out the years with brands like Designersremix, Coster Copenhagen, Bobbi Brown, Ganni, Gucci Beauty, Illum (mixed brands) & Fashion designers from the Royal Academy.
